About Us

Parmagianos has been a staple of Brooklyn since the owner, Papa Parmagiano, opened its doors in 1992. With a passion for great Italian food, Papa Parmigiano wanted to bring the taste of Italy to New York. Using the freshest ingredients he could find, Papa Parmigiano brougt his favorite dishes to your neighborhood.
